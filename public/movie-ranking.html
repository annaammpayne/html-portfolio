Anna's favourite movies

My top 3 movies of all time

Fight Club

I love the alter ego, consumer frustration and daily grind in vain messages of the movie. And of course, one of the best Brad Pitt and Edward Norton performances. A must-see.

The Birds

Alfred Hitchcock. A master. This movie, Psycho and Vertigo are all masterpieces.

American Psycho

A film that satirises 1980s yuppie culture and consumerism. I consider Christian Bale's portrayal of Patrick Bateman as one of his best performances ever.
